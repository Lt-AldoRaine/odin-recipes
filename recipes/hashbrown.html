Hashbrowns

Description

These are classic diner style hashbrowns that are easy and quick to prepare. This simple recipe can be enjoyed anytime in the day but are best as a side for a good breakfast meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 russet potatoes, peeled
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • Pinch of paprika to taste

Steps

  1. Fill large bowl with cold water, and shred potatoes into the bowl. Stir until the water becomes cloudy then drain. Fill bowl with fresh cold water and stir again to dissolve excess starch. Drain the potatoes well, pat dry with paper towels, and squeeze out excess moisture.
  2. Heat butter in large non-stick pan over medium heat. Sprinkle the potatoes into the hot butter and season with salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and paprika.
  3. Cook for 5 minutes or until a brown crust forms on the bottom of the potatoes. Continue to stir until the potatoes are browned all over.
