Honey Mustard Grilled Chicken

Description

Simple yet delicious grilled chicken recipe that is easy and quick to prepare. A healthy recipe you can make for yourself or multiple people during any bbq or get together.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp steak sauce
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to medium heat.
  2. Mix the mustard, honey, mayonnaise, and steak sauce in a shallow bowl. Set aside some honey and mustard for basting, and coat the chicken breasts with the sauce.
  3. Lightly oil the grill. Grill the chicken over indirect heat for 20 minutes, occasionally turning, or until the juices run clear. Occasionally baste with the reserved honey and mustard during the last 10 minutes.
